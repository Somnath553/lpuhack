About Us

Body Toner was founded in 2018 as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality Healthy and nutritional food at great prices. Instead of being just another gym equipment retailer, our founders wanted to be the best in the industry and set their minds to doing so! We provide over two thousand fitness classes a month, with over 500 onsite certified instructors in all the exercise arts. We provide personal training, physical therapy, spa services and more. Over the last two decades Gym and Fitness has grown into one of Australia’s largest online fitness equipment retailers, helping thousands of customers live longer, happier and healthier lives.
